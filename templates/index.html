Vehicle Damage Assessment System

A vehicle damage assessment system is a technology-driven solution used by insurance companies, auto repair shops, and vehicle owners to evaluate and document damages sustained in accidents or incidents. It employs various tools such as digital imaging, computer vision, and machine learning algorithms to analyze and quantify the extent of damage to a vehicle quickly and accurately. This system helps streamline the claims process, reduce human error, and ensure fair and efficient settlements for all parties involved in vehicle-related incidents.
